### Firmware V2 is coming
I am currently working on a **new software version (Firmware V2)**. Your lamp has a safe OTA (Over-The-Air) update feature, which means that as soon as I finish my work, you will be able to upload the new features yourself without having to send the device back.

> **Important note:** I am working on this entire project single-handedly – from the electronics and code, to the mechanics. Creating a dedicated mobile app and stable software is a time-consuming process, so thank you for your patience and support!

---

## What is planned for the new version?

The V2 update will bring a variety of new features:

* **Bluetooth Connectivity:** Full integration with a dedicated mobile app.
* **Animation Configuration:** The ability to add and remove effects, as well as precisely adjust their parameters (brightness, speed) and transition times.
* **Remote Control:** Manage your lamp directly from your smartphone.
* **Weekly Schedule:** Set exact hours for the lamp to automatically turn on and off on specific days of the week.
* **Custom Animations:** A mode that allows you to create your own light compositions.
